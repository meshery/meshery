patternCaveats: |
  -%20**Cost%3A**%20This%20architecture%20utilizes%20several%20managed%20Azure%20services%20(AKS%2C%20ACR%2C%20Azure%20DB%20for%20MySQL)%20which%20will%20incur%20costs.%20Review%20the%20pricing%20for%20each%20service%20and%20configure%20tiers%20appropriate%20for%20your%20workload.%0A-%20**Configuration%3A**%20Component%20configurations%20(e.g.%2C%20Jenkins%20pipelines%2C%20Istio%20traffic%20policies%2C%20Prometheus%20scrape%20targets)%20are%20placeholders%20and%20must%20be%20tailored%20to%20your%20specific%20application's%20needs.%0A-%20**Secrets%20Management%3A**%20Secrets%20(database%20credentials%2C%20registry%20keys%2C%20etc.)%20should%20be%20managed%20securely%20using%20a%20solution%20like%20Azure%20Key%20Vault%20integrated%20with%20AKS%2C%20rather%20than%20being%20hardcoded.%0A-%20**Resource%20Requirements%3A**%20Running%20the%20full%20Istio%20control%20plane%2C%20Jenkins%2C%20and%20a%20Prometheus%2FGrafana%20stack%20requires%20a%20moderately%20sized%20Kubernetes%20cluster.%20Ensure%20your%20AKS%20node%20pools%20have%20sufficient%20CPU%20and%20memory%20resources.
